Laser Focus Disc & Co. Ecommerce Page

Created a front-end e-commerce mock-up project inspired by my love for old-school physical media from the late '90s to early 2000s. Built with React.js and Vite.js, the site pays tribute to the golden era of LaserDisc collecting by showcasing rare movie titles in a clean, retro-themed shopping experience. Focused on dynamic product listings, a mock shopping cart system, and a responsive design to capture the nostalgia while delivering a smooth, modern user experience. (Note: The movies page selections may take a few seconds to load as part of the demo setup.)

Highlights:

  • Built with React.js (using Vite.js for faster development and optimization).
  • Tribute project celebrating late '90s to early 2000s physical media culture.
  • Fully responsive design optimized for mobile and desktop.
  • Honest disclaimer about minor loading delay on movies page.
  • Focused on front-end development and user experience design.

When everything’s done, we do a final review and make any last adjustments before delivery. Clear communication and collaboration are the foundation of the whole thing.
